Things to do

There are a lot of fun things to do and see while you're in The Land.

Browns vs. Steelers Football Game

For those traveling earlier in the week, the Steelers are in town and are playing the Browns in a Thursday night football game! The Hilton is a five-minute walk from the stadium. Who knows, you may see us at the game!

If you are looking for a bar to watch the game, here are a few great options: Map Room, The Clevelander, Corner Alley, Pioneer, and Collision Bend Brewing Company.

Rock & Roll Hall of Fame

The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame is a museum and hall of fame that celebrates the history of rock music, and all of the artists, producers, engineers, and other people who have left their mark on the rock and roll world. Located within a 5-10 minute walk from the Hilton!

West Side Market

The West Side Market is the oldest operating indoor/outdoor market space in Cleveland. It is located at the corner of West 25th Street and Loraine Avenue in the Ohio City neighborhood.

Cleveland Arcade

The Cleveland Arcade is a historic Cleveland landmark and is where David proposed back in April of 2023!

Cleveland Museum of Art

The Cleveland Museum of Art is renowned for the quality and breadth of its collection, which includes more than 63,000 artworks and spans 6,000 years of achievement in the arts. It is in the Wade Park District.

The Corner Alley

The Corner Alley is a sleek and modern bowling alley, located conveniently on East 4th Street, in downtown Cleveland.

Forest City Shuffleboard Arena & Bar

Nestled in Ohio City, Forest City Shuffleboard Arena and Bar offers a fun atmosphere for friends and family to get a little active and competitive while playing shuffleboard!

Places to eat

East 4th Street

East 4th Street is like no other street in the world! Offering many different food, entertainment, and night life spots in the heart of downtown Cleveland.

Flats East Bank

One of Cleveland premier entertainment districts, located off Lake Erie on the bank of the Cuyahoga River. Many great night life opportunities: Good Night John Boy, Welcome to the Farm, The Big Bang and Beerhead. In addition to some great restaurants as well: Alley Cat, Lindey’s Lake House & Lago.

Astoria Café & Market

Astoria Café & Market is one of our favorites! It is one of the Klonaris cousins' restaurants. It is a sleek Mediterranean market and eatery offering gourmet meats and cheeses, plus tapas, wine, and cocktails. In 2022 Guy Fieri visited to feature them on Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives. It is also where Diana gets all her chickpea supplies for The Chick and the Pea!

Barrio in Tremont

Looking for one of the best tacos in Cleveland, Barrio in Tremont is a must. The best part is that everything is customizable and they operate off of a build your own style.

Yours Truly

If you have ever heard a fellow Clevelander mention “Natso Fries”, you must stop in Yours Truly, specifically for Brunch. Classic diner and every Clevelanders favorite.

Collision Bend Brewing Company

Collision Bend Brewing Company is an artisanal brewery and full-service restaurant, featuring crisp, bright beers brewed on site, served alongside a menu inspired by Cleveland’s melting pot. Located in the Flats East Bank.

Little Italy

Little Italy is a historic and cultural neighborhood in Cleveland, Ohio that's known for the best Italian food in the city. Some restaurants to check out: Mama Santa, La Dolce Vita, Mia Bella & Michealangelo’s.
